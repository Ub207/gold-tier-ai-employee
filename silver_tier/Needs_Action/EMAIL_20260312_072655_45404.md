Accelerate Your AI Agents With Nemotron 3 Super

The next member of the Nemotron™ 3 family is here. Nemotron 3 Super, a new open model that further expands the transparent, efficient foundation for building specialized agentic AI across industries, is now available.



Like the entire model family, Nemotron 3 Super leverages a breakthrough hybrid mixture-of-experts (MoE) architecture, high-quality open datasets, and reinforcement learning environments to deliver state-of-the-art 

accuracy to build high-throughput agents.



Artificial Analysis, an independent benchmarking organization, ranks Nemotron 3 as the most open and efficient model in its class, with leading accuracy on key coding, reasoning, and agentic benchmarks.



Nemotron 3 Super is available now with ready-to-use cookbooks. With Nemotron 3 Nano already released and Ultra to follow, developers have a full range of open and efficient models to build the next generation of AI agents.
