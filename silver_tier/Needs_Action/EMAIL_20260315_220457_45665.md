###🎉 RemNote 1.24 - Learn Any PDF In Half the Time



RemNote 1.24 introduces a new "Learn PDF" experience. It's the fastest and least stressful way to learn any topic. 





Upload any PDF, and the AI Tutor will immediately organize your PDF into sections and key topics. For each section, learn with **high-quality flashcards, quizzes, summaries, study guides, interactive tutor questions, and more**.





[ **Try the New "Learn PDF" Tab**](https://r.broadcasts.remnote.com/mk/cl/f/sh/WCPzyXJTZ6uvcxCbWDmNhweJiBJqTlBM/Q5wW20adP2rh)





###🧠 Organized Sections & Mastery Tracking





The AI Tutor organizes your PDF or other file into a set of organized sections. Each section is designed to be “bite-sized” and learnable in short study sessions.



As you study, the AI Tutor tracks mastery of every key topic, and helps you get to 100%.





###📃 Summaries





Each section begins with a comprehensive summary. Learn with clear explanations, examples, images, underlined key terms, interactive questions, and more. 





###🗂️ Flashcards





Memorize with high-quality flashcards. Cards are organized by priority, linked to your underlying PDF, and designed for easy mastery.





###❓ Quizzes





To ensure you're ready for exam day, practice with multiple choice and free response questions. Choose between easier "recall" quizzes or harder "application" quizzes. 





For incorrect answers, the tutor will offer clear explanations and additional practice questions.





[ **Get Started: Upload a File**](https://r.broadcasts.remnote.com/mk/cl/f/sh/WCPzyXJTZ72igzPrsJG3Tgo6qyCxjSnU/6pDt0EW3X2Dm)

Access a comprehensive study guide from the “Summary” tab. The guide identifies and shows:





- 📖 Core Concepts 

- ⚠️ Common Misunderstandings 

- 🗂️ Exam Traps 

- and more





###➡️ Import Existing Questions to RemNote





Upload a PDF with existing questions or flashcards, and convert them to RemNote flashcards in one click.
