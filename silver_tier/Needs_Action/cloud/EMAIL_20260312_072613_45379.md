### Body (preview)

This pull request adds opt-in runner-managed model retries across the Agents SDK. It introduces `ModelSettings.retry` and exported retry policy helpers, routes both non-streaming and streaming model execution through shared retry orchestration, and teaches the OpenAI Responses, Chat Completions, websocket, and LiteLLM adapters to surface retry advice while forcing provider-managed retries off on replay attempts. Existing behavior stays unchanged unless callers opt in to the new retry settings.



It also preserves session and server-conversation rewind behavior during retries, merges inherited retry settings during `ModelSettings.resolve()`, and adds retry examples plus broad regression coverage for serialization, usage accounting, provider vetoes, replay safety, and streamed responses.



see also: https://github.com/openai/openai-agents-js/pull/1083
