## _ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE_
🌎 Anthropic Is Systematically Eating OpenClaw

Here's what happened. Claude Code shipped a new flag called --channels as a research preview. It lets MCP servers push messages into your Claude Code session while you're away. CI results, Slack messages, monitoring alerts, chat messages from any platform. Claude sees them and reacts.

That was OpenClaw's entire pitch. A persistent AI agent wired into your messaging apps that runs 24/7 and responds to you wherever you are. OpenClaw had 15+ platform integrations. Claude had zero. That gap just closed.
