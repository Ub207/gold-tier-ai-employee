## _ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE_

🌎 Nemotron 3 Super: NVIDIA Just Entered The Model Race For Real



Most people think of NVIDIA as the chip company that everyone else depends on. Today they reminded the industry they are also a serious model company.



Nemotron 3 Super is a 120 billion total, 12 billion active parameter model built on a hybrid Mamba-Transformer mixture-of-experts architecture. That gap between total and active parameters is the whole point, the model activates only what it needs, which is how NVIDIA delivers over 5x higher throughput than the previous Nemotron Super while maintaining frontier-level accuracy.
