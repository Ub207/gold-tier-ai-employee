499 unread emails. Zero staff. One AI agent cleared it in minutes.

Here's what we built for a solo founder last week:

A Python-based AI employee that runs 24/7 — monitoring Gmail, WhatsApp, and LinkedIn simultaneously.

When an email arrives, the AI reads it, triages it, and writes 3 draft reply options. The founder picks one, moves a file, and it sends. No manual typing. No missed follow-ups.

The entire inbox — 499 emails — got triaged, categorised, and draft replies generated. Turns out 90% were GitHub notifications (auto-archived). The remaining 10% had draft replies ready within minutes.

The system also:
→ Monitors WhatsApp and drafts replies
→ Schedules LinkedIn posts with rate limit enforcement
→ Generates a CEO briefing every Monday morning
→ Creates draft invoices in Odoo ERP

Total setup time: 1 day.
Time saved per week: 8–12 hours.

This is not a SaaS product. It's a custom-built AI employee, trained on your business rules, running on your own machine.

If you're a solo founder drowning in admin work, this is worth a conversation.
